# AI Passport — Manifesto

> A compass for everyone contributing to this project.  
> For mission and north star, see [VISION.md](VISION.md).

1. **AI identity belongs to the user.** Not OpenAI. Not Anthropic. Not Cursor. The user.

2. **AI providers are guests, not owners.** They read with permission. They never own the passport.

3. **Privacy is the default.** No grant, no data. Revocation is immediate.

4. **Permissions are explicit.** Every section shared must be deliberately approved.

5. **Open specification over vendor lock-in.** Format is documented. Anyone can implement.

6. **Local-first whenever possible.** The passport lives on the user's device before the cloud.

7. **Interoperability over ecosystem isolation.** One identity works across AI systems.

8. **Security before convenience.** Encryption and scoped access are non-negotiable.

---

*One identity. Every AI.*
